Simple launch methodology

Two scores. One shared framework.

Businesses are scored on evidence. Travelers are scored on verified actions. Both use five Impact Dimensions and a maximum score of 100.

For businesses

Business Impact Score

Each dimension receives 0, 5, 10, 15 or 20 points based on the strength of available evidence.

0No evidence5Commitment10Documented practice15Strong repeated practice20Measured or independently supported evidence
Launch status

Business scores are provisional until reviewed with the business. Missing evidence receives zero; this does not automatically mean poor performance.

For travelers

Traveler Impact Score

Verified actions earn points in one of the five dimensions. Each dimension is capped at 20 points.

Participate · +2Visit, attend or support an approved partner.Contribute · +5Volunteer or join a meaningful activity.Lead · +10Organize, host or lead an approved action.
Simple rules

Actions must be verified, and the same action can only be counted once. Badges recognize milestones but do not add points.
