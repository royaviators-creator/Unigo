UNIGO methodology

One clear impact framework.

Businesses are scored on evidence of their practices. Travelers are scored on verified actions. Both use the same five Impact Dimensions and a maximum score of 100.

CRS

Carbon Reduction

Energy, waste, mobility, circularity, sourcing and resource efficiency.

GES

Gender Equality

Women-led ownership, leadership, equality commitments and documented practices.

LGBTQIS

LGBTQ+ Inclusion

Policies, partnerships, training, safe-space commitments and community support.

LES

Local Economy

Local sourcing, employment, ownership and local value creation.

CIS

Community Impact

Education, volunteering, cultural exchange, accessibility and community programmes.

Business Impact Score

Evidence of business practice.

BIS = CRS + GES + LGBTQIS + LES + CIS. Each dimension is worth 20 points.

Business evidence rule

UNIGO scores only evidence published by the business or submitted for review. Missing evidence receives zero, but zero does not mean poor performance. Scores are provisional until verified.

Traveler Impact Score

A simple score designed for a long-term journey.

Traveler Impact Score = capped CRS + capped GES + capped LGBTQIS + capped LES + capped CIS. Each dimension is capped at 20 points, so the maximum score is 100.

Participate · 2 pointsVerified purchase, visit, attendance or short approved activityContribute · 5 pointsMeaningful participation, volunteering, workshop or completed impact experienceLead · 10 pointsOrganising, facilitating, mentoring or leading a verified impact action
Traveler evidence rule

A receipt, ticket, partner confirmation, QR check-in or participation record is required. One action may support more than one relevant dimension only when the partner defines this in advance. The same evidence can only be used once.

Badge rule

Badges recognise milestones and do not add points to the Traveler Impact Score. Lifetime counts continue growing even after a dimension or the overall score reaches its cap.

Shared safeguards

Transparent and practical.

No invented outcomes

UNIGO does not estimate CO₂ savings, beneficiaries or economic value without credible evidence.

No duplicate scoring

The same evidence cannot be counted twice.

Clear separation

Business scores assess organisations. Traveler scores record personal actions.

Reviewable

Scores may be updated when better evidence is provided.
