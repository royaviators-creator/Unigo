Simple point rules

Small actions start the journey. Leadership completes it.

Participate · +2

A verified purchase, visit, event attendance or short approved activity.

Contribute · +5

Meaningful participation, volunteering, a workshop or a completed impact experience.

Lead · +10

Organising, facilitating, mentoring or leading a verified impact action.

The math

Traveler Impact Score = capped CRS + capped GES + capped LGBTQIS + capped LES + capped CIS. Each dimension is capped at 20 points, so the maximum score is 100.

Long-term journey rules

Repetition alone cannot produce a perfect score.

Dimension cap

Each dimension contributes a maximum of 20 points.

Partner diversity

Only the first two Participate actions from the same partner can add score in one dimension.

Leadership requirement

Reaching 20 in a dimension requires at least one verified Lead action in that dimension.

Balanced profile

A score of 100 requires 20 points in all five dimensions and at least one Lead action in each.

Badge rule

Badges recognise milestones. They do not add points to the Traveler Impact Score.

Safeguards

Simple enough to use. Credible enough to trust.

Verified action first

A receipt, ticket, QR check-in, partner confirmation or participation record is required.

No duplicate scoring

The same evidence can only be counted once.

Predefined dimensions

An action may support more than one dimension only when the partner defines this before participation.

No invented outcomes

UNIGO does not convert participation into environmental or social outcomes without credible evidence.
