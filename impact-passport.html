Progress

Badges

Recognition

Traveler Level

Access

Your overall level can qualify you for offers created directly by participating businesses.

Example

A café may offer a free drink to travelers with the Community Builder badge. UNIGO only verifies eligibility and displays the offer.

Safeguards

Simple enough to use. Credible enough to trust.

Verified action first

Score growth requires suitable evidence such as a receipt, ticket, partner confirmation, or participation record.

No invented outcomes

UNIGO will not translate participation into environmental or social outcomes without a defensible methodology.

No duplicate rewards

The same action cannot be repeatedly submitted for additional score growth.

Separate systems

Traveler Impact Score shows personal progress; Business Impact Score assesses organizations.
