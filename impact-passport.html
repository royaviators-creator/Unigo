Reward framework

Impact Credits will follow verified effort.

Awareness

Learning, pledging, or completing a short educational action.

Participation

Booking, attending, purchasing locally, volunteering, or joining a verified activity.

Leadership

Organizing, teaching, mentoring, leading a campaign, or creating sustained community value.

Exact credit values are intentionally not displayed until the official Impact Credit economy is approved.

Safeguards

Simple enough to use. Credible enough to trust.

Verified action first

Credits require suitable evidence such as a receipt, ticket, partner confirmation, or participation record.

No invented outcomes

UNIGO will not translate participation into environmental or social outcomes without a defensible methodology.

No duplicate rewards

The same action cannot be repeatedly submitted for additional credits.

Separate systems

Impact Credits reward traveler actions; Business Impact Scores assess organizations.
