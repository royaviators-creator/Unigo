Simple traveler framework

Three levels of action.

Tier 1 · Awareness

1–5 IC

Reading, watching, pledging or completing a short learning action.

Tier 2 · Participation

10–20 IC

Booking, attending, purchasing locally, volunteering or joining a verified activity.

Tier 3 · Leadership

50–75 IC

Organising, teaching, mentoring, leading a campaign or creating a sustained community initiative.

Pilot safeguards

Simple enough to test. Credible enough to trust.

Verified action first

Credits require a receipt, ticket, partner confirmation, participation record or other suitable evidence.

No invented outcomes

UNIGO does not convert attendance into kilograms of CO₂ or euros of local impact without a defensible methodology.

No duplicate rewards

The same action cannot be repeatedly submitted for additional credits.

Separate systems

Traveler Impact Credits reward actions; Business Impact Scores assess organisations.
