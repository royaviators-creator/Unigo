Product direction

Recognition should follow verified participation.

Impact Score

Progress

A future Passport can show how verified actions contribute across the five UNIGO dimensions.

Badges

Recognition

Badges can represent meaningful milestones without becoming a currency or financial balance.

Traveler Level

Access

Future levels may help participating businesses offer relevant access or benefits directly.

These elements describe the intended product model. They are not yet active account features or awarded rewards.

Safeguards

Simple enough to use. Credible enough to trust.

Verified action first

Future progress should require suitable evidence such as a receipt, ticket, partner confirmation, or participation record.

No invented outcomes

UNIGO will not translate participation into environmental or social outcomes without a defensible methodology.

No duplicate recognition

The same activity should not be repeatedly submitted for additional progress.

Separate systems

Traveler participation and Business Impact Scores must remain distinct.
