How it works

Three simple action types.

Participate · +2

Visit, attend or support an approved partner.

Contribute · +5

Volunteer or join a meaningful impact activity.

Lead · +10

Organise, host or lead an approved action.

Simple launch logic

Your score grows through verified actions across the five Impact Dimensions. Each dimension contributes up to 20 points, for a maximum score of 100.
