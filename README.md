# UNIGO

Bringing Cultures Together

Travel Light. Impact Big.

Official repository for the UNIGO launch platform.

## Launch language

The public website should present UNIGO as a live, credible platform. Do not use public Beta wording.

## Founding Community

UNIGO includes two founding groups:

- Founding Travelers: early travelers with founding recognition, Impact Passport access, referral Impact Credits and a direct feedback channel.
- Founding Businesses: approved responsible businesses with founding recognition, impact profiles, launch visibility and referral recognition.

## Referral Impact Credits

Referral Impact Credits should reward real activation only:

- Traveler rewards unlock after successful onboarding.
- Business rewards unlock after approval and profile completion.
- Admin should be able to configure rewards, prevent abuse and export referral analytics.
