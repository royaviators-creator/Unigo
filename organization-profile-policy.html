Organization Profile & Corrections Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026. Drafted for final legal review.

Public information

UNIGO may create provisional organization profiles using information published by the organization or other public sources.

No implied relationship

A provisional profile does not mean that the organization is a UNIGO partner, customer, sponsor, or endorsed organization.

Corrections and claims

Authorized representatives may request a correction, clarification, profile claim, or removal. UNIGO may ask for reasonable evidence of authority before making material changes.

Review process

UNIGO aims to review requests fairly, update inaccurate information, and clearly distinguish public-information reviews from confirmed partner profiles.

Contact

Send requests to profiles@unigo.now and include the organization name, requested change, supporting source, and your relationship to the organization.
