This policy explains how UNIGO, operated by Royaviators in the Netherlands, uses cookies and similar technologies on the website.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies and local storage may be used to support core website functions, form integrity, referral continuity, security and basic navigation. These are required for the website to work reliably.

Analytics Cookies

UNIGO may use analytics cookies or similar technologies to understand aggregate website performance, page usage and important CTA interactions. Analytics helps improve reliability, content quality and launch readiness.

Cookie Preferences

When UNIGO introduces a cookie preference banner or settings panel, your preferences will be used to control optional cookies where required. Essential cookies may remain active because they are necessary for the site to function.

Browser Controls

You can block, delete or restrict cookies through your browser settings. Some parts of the website may not work as intended if essential storage is disabled.

Future Cookie Banner Compatibility

This policy is designed to support a future cookie banner and preference center. When those controls are added, this page will remain the reference for cookie categories and user choices.
