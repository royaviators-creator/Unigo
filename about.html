ABOUT UNIGO

Bringing Cultures Together

UNIGO helps travelers discover meaningful experiences, support ethical businesses, earn Impact Credits, and create measurable positive impact.

Our Mission

Transform tourism into a force for sustainability, inclusion, and local prosperity.

Why UNIGO Exists

Modern tourism often leaves little value behind. UNIGO helps travelers support local communities, reduce environmental impact, promote inclusion, and track their contribution through an Impact Passport.

How It Works

  • Discover Impact Experiences
  • Participate in local initiatives
  • Earn Impact Credits
  • Build your Impact Passport
  • Join the Impact Movement

Impact Areas

  • Carbon Reduction (CRS)
  • Gender Equality (GES)
  • LGBTQ+ Inclusion (LGBTQIS)
  • Local Economy Support (LES)
