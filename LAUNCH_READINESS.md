# UNIGO Launch Readiness

## P0 before launch

- Present UNIGO as a live platform rather than a public beta.
- Use `Join UNIGO` for travelers and `Join as a Partner` for organizations.
- Verify every public partnership and traction claim.
- Clearly label illustrative scores and profiles.
- Test traveler and partner forms end to end.
- Confirm privacy, consent, mobile navigation, metadata and social previews.

## Launch gate

Launch only when all P0 items pass on mobile and desktop and a rollback path is documented.
