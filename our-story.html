Why UNIGO exists

Travel should create connection, not distance.

UNIGO began with one simple question: what if every journey could leave a place, a community and a traveler better than before?

Travel can bring cultures together, strengthen local economies and open people to new perspectives. But too often, the positive impact of a trip is difficult to see, compare or continue.

UNIGO was created to make that impact more visible. It connects travelers with meaningful experiences, ethical businesses and local initiatives, then turns those choices into a simple record of participation and progress.

The goal is not to make travel perfect. The goal is to make better choices easier, clearer and more rewarding.

The problem

Good intentions are not enough.

Travelers lack clarity

People want to support responsible businesses, but often cannot tell which claims are credible.

Impact is fragmented

Sustainability, inclusion, local value and community contribution are usually presented separately.

Local initiatives lack visibility

Smaller ethical businesses and facilitators are often harder to discover than large mainstream options.

The UNIGO approach

One platform. Clearer choices. Measurable participation.

Discover

Find experiences and businesses connected to sustainability, inclusion and local communities.

Understand

See transparent, evidence-based provisional business scores rather than unsupported claims.

Participate

Join activities that create cultural, environmental and social value.

Build an Impact Passport

Record verified actions, earn Impact Credits and develop a personal impact identity over time.

Our direction

From a local pilot to a wider movement.

The first UNIGO pilot focuses on Utrecht, Amsterdam and Rotterdam. It starts small: a transparent scoring framework, a limited group of businesses and a clear beta experience for travelers.

From there, the platform can grow through evidence, feedback and real participation. The long-term vision is a trusted impact travel ecosystem where travelers, businesses and communities all benefit from better visibility and better choices.
