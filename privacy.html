Privacy Policy

UNIGO is operated by Royaviators in the Netherlands. This policy explains how we handle personal data when you use the website, join UNIGO, contact us, or apply as a partner.

Data Controller

Royaviators is the data controller for personal data processed through UNIGO. Privacy requests can be sent to hello@unigo.now.

Information Collected

  • Contact details such as name, email address, city or country and organization.
  • Messages, application details and preferences submitted through UNIGO forms.
  • Technical information such as IP-derived request data, browser information, pages visited and basic analytics events.
  • Consent records and operational metadata needed to protect the service from misuse.

Why Data Is Collected

We use personal data to respond to inquiries, manage community and partner interest, review applications, protect the website, improve performance and understand how visitors use UNIGO.

Legal Basis Under GDPR

  • Consent, when you submit a form or choose to receive communications.
  • Legitimate interests, including website security, analytics, service improvement and responding to business inquiries.
  • Pre-contractual steps, when you apply as a business, facilitator or community partner.
  • Legal obligations, where record keeping or compliance is required by law.

Cookies

UNIGO may use essential cookies or local storage required for basic website functionality. Analytics cookies may be used to understand site performance. More detail is available in the Cookie Policy.

Analytics

UNIGO uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate website usage, important CTA clicks and form submissions. Analytics is used to improve reliability and content quality, not to sell personal information.

Data Retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, including responding to inquiries, managing applications, complying with legal duties and maintaining security records. You may request deletion when retention is no longer required.

Third-Party Services

UNIGO may rely on trusted service providers for hosting, database storage, analytics, email handling and security. These providers process data only as needed to support UNIGO operations.

User Rights

Under GDPR, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability or objection to processing. You may withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority.

Contact for Privacy Requests

For privacy questions, rights requests or general support, contact hello@unigo.now.
